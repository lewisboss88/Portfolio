About Me

Hello there!

I'm Simao, and my life's journey is a colourful fusion of cultures and a deep-seated love for technology. I started my adventure in the vibrant land of Angola, where the rhythms of life are as diverse as the people.

However, it was Italy that nurtured me through my formative years. It's in the heart of this beautiful country that I not only grew up but also honed my skills as an electronic technician, navigating the intricate world of circuits and components.

Over a decade ago, I embarked on a new chapter in the United Kingdom, where I've continued to call home. Here, I pursued my passion for technology further by completing an undergraduate degree in computing. This was the turning point that led me into the captivating world of software development.

Nowadays a freelancer, I've carved my niche as a frontend developer. I take pride in crafting visually stunning and user-friendly interfaces, making websites and applications come to life with a touch of creativity and functionality. In the meantime, I'm steadily venturing into the realms of backend and full-stack development, eager to tackle the intricate challenges of building robust digital experiences.

My multicultural background, technical prowess, and insatiable curiosity have sculpted me into a versatile developer. I'm here not just to write code, but to create digital wonders and bring innovative solutions to life. So, whether it's designing the perfect user interface or delving deep into the backend, I'm Simao, and I'm here to make the digital world a better, more personal place, one line of code at a time.
